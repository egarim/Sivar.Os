## 🎤 Q&A Talking Points

### "How is this different from Instagram/Facebook?"

> "Great question. Instagram is for content. We're for *transactions*. They post photos. We let you BOOK the photographer. We're where discovery meets action."

### "What about payment processing?"

> "Phase 1: Deposits via bank transfer (what businesses do now). Phase 2: Integrated payments (Stripe/PayPal). Phase 3: Crypto for zero fees. We adapt to what Salvadorans actually use."

### "Why will businesses switch from WhatsApp?"

> "They won't! We INTEGRATE with WhatsApp. Customers can book via WhatsApp. Businesses get notifications via WhatsApp. We enhance what they already use, not replace it."

### "What's your monetization?"

> "Three tiers:
> - Free: Basic profile, manual bookings
> - Pro ($20/month): Automated bookings, analytics, priority support
> - Enterprise ($100/month): Multiple locations, team management, API access
> 
> Plus: 2% transaction fee on online payments (industry standard)."

### "How do you handle no-shows?"

> "Deposits! We require 20-30% deposit to confirm bookings. Plus automated reminders 24h before. No-show rate in beta: less than 2%."

### "What about internet access in rural areas?"

> "Mobile-first design works on 3G. WhatsApp works everywhere. We built for El Salvador's reality, not Silicon Valley's."
